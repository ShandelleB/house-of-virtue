Mission Statement

The House of Virtue is a faith based ministry that follows Biblical principles. Our purpose is to provide a safe haven for women with children and/or pregnant and to help free them from addictions by building a relationship with Jesus Christ. Our goal is to help the mother deliver a drug free, healthy newborn and to "train up a child in the way it should go." ~Proverbs 22:6

The House of Virtue Offers:

  2. Overcomers
  3. Celebrate Recovery

The House of Virtue Facts:

All of our residents will complete a 90 day Overcomers program.

Residents are required to attend two church services a week.

Each resident will have a personalized schedule that is designed to bring about the most effective success in their lives as well as their children.

All residents are subject to random drug screening.
